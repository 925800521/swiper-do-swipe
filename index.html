swiper-do-swipe.js v0.5

(Webkit/Firefox only, for now)

Demo: swipe the page left or right, or up and down (touch or mouse).

Features:

  • it's fast (using requestAnimationFrame, fast clicking, and other optimisations)
  • live animation feedback during swipe (not just a 'completed swipe' animation like jQuery Mobile et al)
  • designed to be extensible with other swipe animations
  • has callback for page turning that you can tie into your mobile app
  • no dependencies

Known issues: scrollbar size is a bit wrong. will fix for next version
