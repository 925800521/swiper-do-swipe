swiper-do-swipe.js v0.4

(Webkit only, for now)

Demo: swipe the page left or right (touch or mouse).

Features:

  • it's fast (using requestAnimationFrame and other optimisations)
  • live animation feedback during swipe (not just a 'completed swipe' animation like jQuery Mobile et al)
  • designed to be extensible with other swipe animations

(vertical scrolling not really supported yet, but soon...)
